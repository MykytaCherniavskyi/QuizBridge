Quick Setup

To get the most out of QuizBridge, pin it to your toolbar:

  1. Click the puzzle piece icon in your Chrome toolbar
  2. Find "QuizBridge" in the dropdown
  3. Click the pin icon next to QuizBridge

Now you'll have quick access to QuizBridge whenever you need it!

Getting Started

With QuizBridge, you can:

  • Select text on any webpage to save words
  • Add definitions to your saved words
  • Sync your words and definitions with Quizlet flashcards
